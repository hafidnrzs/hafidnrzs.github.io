Story

Yato is an Oni combat personnel of Rhodes Island part of Op A4 along with Noir Corne and Rangers. All about her past is that she was once an assassin conscripted during the Higashi civil war who defeated Noir Corne on a particular mission, and Yato prefers to keep this past in low profile. The two eventually found their way to R.I. after a hilarious, "romantic" moment when they decided to desert the army after sparing their target and flee the war-torn country.

Aside from her years of combat experience both during the war and in R.I., Yato is known for her light-sensitive skin that makes hard to withstand sunlight. As a matter of fact, she has been wearing her mask all the time. While many are curious about her reason, only Noir Corne understands her. Nevertheless, there will be a day when Yato finally takes off her mask and braves herself against her own weakness.

Yato, wearing the Kirin R armor, is one of the protagonists of A Flurry to the Flame.

Trivia

  • Her name means "night blade" in Japanese. Specifically, she is named after the Yato-no-kami (Kanji: 夜刀の神), a horned snake deity in Japanese folklore appearing in the Hitachi no Kuni Fudoki which is said to bring familial extermination to anyone witnessing them, hence being called an araburu-kami (Kanji: 荒ぶる神, lit. "malevolent god").
    • In the Elite 2 artwork of her alternate version, a snake with two horns appears in the bottom right of the background, which is exactly a Yato-no-kami.
  • Yato is the first Operator of the 2★ class who has a 6★ alternate version.
